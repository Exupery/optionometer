Optionometer

Multi-leg strategy screener for equity options

Optionometer presents trading opportunities by determining which trades would potentially yield the most profit with the greatest chance of expiring at their full profit potential. Optionometer calculates possible trades for all securities in the Dow Jones Industrial Average, S&P 500, and Nasdaq 100. For each stock in these indices the results are calculated for every possible option combination that would result in one of these four strategies:

  • Bull Call Spread
  • Bear Call Spread
  • Bear Put Spread
  • Bull Put Spread

Optionometer analyzes hundreds of thousands of possible strategies and presents the information needed to help you make better trades. As Optionometer is presently in beta end-of-day data is used for all quotes and there may be the occasional bug, please send any feedback to
