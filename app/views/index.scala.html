Optionometer

Multi-leg strategy screener for equity options

Optionometer presents trading opportunities by determining which trades would potentially yield the most profit with the greatest chance of expiring at their full profit potential. Optionometer calculates possible trades for all securities in the Dow Jones Industrial Average, S&P 500, and Nasdaq 100. For each stock in these indices the results are calculated for every possible option combination for these strategies:

  • Bull Call Spread
  • Bear Call Spread
  • Bear Put Spread
  • Bull Put Spread

Optionometer analyzes hundreds of thousands of possible trades and presents the information to help you make better trading decisions. Optionometer is under development and thus delayed quotes are presently used and there may be the occasional bug. Please send any feedback to - for updates follow Optionometer on Twitter or Facebook, happy trading!
